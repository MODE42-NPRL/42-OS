42/OS Installation

There are two archives to extract by user-choice with 7-zip/compatible software:
.tar.7z Archive
and
.hdf.7z Archive

The .tar.7z is the whole Filesystem for direct extracting it to a new media, directory or drive.
First, un7zip the .tar.7z. After: tar xvfp <filename>.tar -C <destination/target> and done or switch
to the target/destination and do the tar xvfp.. without "-C <destination/target>".

The .hdf is emulator and boot ready for Emulators like *UAE out of the box and contains at Data-HDF: the
.tar archive already extracted thus without .7z.


Clone/copy the Filesystem to new Boot-Drive:
There a different ways to bring 42/OS to your MACH/Machine for useage.

Example 1: .tar-Way
"tar xvfp .." direct at the Machine for install/use to a new/fresh Parition/Drive/Media.
Dont forget to set with HDToolBox the Parition/Drive/Media boot-able and boot from it.

Example 2: .hdf-Way
Plug yours Partition/Drive/Media for Installation to a *UAE/etc Machine, configure the
Emulator and boot the .hdf. Manage with SYS:System/Disktools/HDToolBox the Target Partitions and Drive.
After extract from Data-HDF:<filename>-.tar 42/OS to yours Parition/Drive/Media.
After finished, setup via HDToolBox the 42/OS Drive/Parition boot-able with the advanced Options
and boot it.


Note:
- 42/OS uses Smartfilesystem as default Filesystem
