There are two archives to extract by user-choice with 7-zip/compatible software:
.tar.7z Archive
and
.hdf.7z Archive

The .tar.7z is the whole Filesystem for direct extracting it to a media, directory or drive.
First, un7zip the .tar.7z and after: tar xvfp <filename>.tar -C <destination/target> and done.

The .hdf.7z is emulator and boot ready for Emulators like *UAE out of the box and contains at Data-HDF: the
.tar archive already extracted thus without .7z.

Clone/copy the Filesystem to yours Boot-Drive:
There a different ways to bring 42/OS to your MACH/Machine for useage.

Example 1:
"tar xvfp .." direct at the Machine for install/use to a new/fresh Parition/Drive/Media.
Dont forget to set the Parition/Drive/Media boot-able and boot from it.
